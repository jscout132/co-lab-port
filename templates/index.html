Hi, I'm Jacob.
I like birds.

They're everywhere! Once you start learning what to look and listen for, walking through the woods becomes a completely different experience.

I'm originally from Northwest Ohio

I moved to Durham not long before the pandemic and had a hard time adjusting. One of the activites that got me through, like many people, was taking long walks in the woods. During that time, I started to get to know my new home through nature. It made such a difference. I more learned about the flora and fauna of the area the more at home I felt.

Prior to starting the Coding Temple program, I was a fundraiser and grant writer in the non-profit space. I worked a number of years in sexual and domestic violence prevention work, then a couple years in voting rights and justice system reform. I am passionate about building a better and safer world with increased access for those who have historically been left out.
